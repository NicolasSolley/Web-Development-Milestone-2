Department of Information Technology


Welcome to the Department of Information Technology at Kennesaw State University! Our curriculum incorporates various courses from information technology, and computing and software engineering to ensure that our students receive the highest quality education possible. We focus on meeting the needs of IT users within an organizational and societal context through the selection, creation, application, integration and administration of computing technologies.



Our goal is to develop applied research, training, and practicing projects and programs that contribute to the intellectual growth of our students and faculty.



To provide maximum opportunties for faculty and students to become engaged in the research, training, teaching and application of policy and practices on topics geared towards Information Technology.




For more information about the programs we offer, please follow the links included within this website, or feel free to contact us by telephone at 470-578-3803, stop by Atrium Building office J393, or e-mail at itdepartmentinfo@kennesaw.edu. We look forward to seeing you soon!
